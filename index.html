About Me

Hi, my name is Collin O’Connor, I am an aspiring web developer/student at Learn Academy, a coding school where I have learned the tools to become a full stack web developer; using languages such as Javascript, react, ruby, and rails. I am driven by the daily challenges and problem solving coding intails and motivated by the feeling you get once you see your work come to life.

One thing I didn’t realize when I started coding was how much teamwork was involved to get the job done. Working as a team member is something I have found my self enjoying and accustomed to from my experiences playing sports throughout my youth and serving in the military. In the military, our mission was to service Aids to Navigation which involved working with heavy equipment on the unforgiving sea. This is where attention to detail and staying focused on the mission was important skills that I found to be extra be helpful in the development world.

My goals are to acquire a home in the tech industry that has great leadership and mentors where I can grow within the ranks and expand my knowledge to my highest capability.
